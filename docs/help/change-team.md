The app follows the Edmonton Elks by default, but you can point it at any CFL team. Your pick is saved on this device and re-colors the whole app to match.

## Steps

1. Tap the **settings gear** at the bottom of the screen.
2. In the **Team** section, open the dropdown (it shows the team you're currently following).
3. Choose a team from the list.
4. Tap the **✕** to close Settings.

## What you'll see

The app updates right away — the header logo, team name, and colors switch to your chosen team, and the featured game, results, and upcoming sections now follow them. The choice sticks the next time you open the app on this device.
