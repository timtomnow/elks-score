The home screen shows a compact **Game Stats** preview, but the full box score has three tabs so you can dig past the final score into how it was won.

## Steps

1. Tap the **Game Stats** card (it shows a score preview and a **Full box score & play-by-play** hint), or tap any game in **Recent Elks Results**.
2. The box score opens. Use the tabs at the top to switch between **Team Stats**, **Player Stats**, and **Play-by-Play**.
3. **Team Stats** compares both teams on total / passing / rushing yards, first downs, 3rd-down efficiency, time of possession, penalties, and turnovers.
4. **Player Stats** lists every player by category for both teams, not just the leaders.
5. Tap the **✕** (or swipe the sheet down) to close.

## What you'll see

A bottom sheet with the score up top and the three stat tabs below. If a particular game has no stats available yet, the app says so rather than showing an empty tab.
