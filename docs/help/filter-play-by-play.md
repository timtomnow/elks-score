The **Play-by-Play** tab lists every play of the game, grouped by quarter with the most recent first. Filters let you jump straight to the moments you care about.

## Before you start

- Open a game's box score and switch to the **Play-by-Play** tab — see [Open a game's box score](/help/open-box-score).

## Steps

1. Use the top row of buttons to filter by **quarter** — tap one or more quarters to show only those.
2. Use the second row to filter by play type: **All**, **Scoring**, **Sacks**, or **Turnovers**.
3. Tap more than one filter to combine them; tap an active filter again to turn it off.
4. Tap **All** to clear the play-type filter and show every play again.

## What you'll see

The play list updates immediately to match your selection. Scoring plays are highlighted so they stand out as you scroll.
