Elks Score puts the most relevant Elks game first, then everything else around the league, on a single scrolling screen. This tour shows you what each part is.

## Steps

1. Open the app. The top **featured game** card shows the most relevant Elks game — a live game if one is on, otherwise the next scheduled game, otherwise the most recent final.
2. Tap **Game details** on that card to expand the round label, kickoff time, and (while live) timeouts remaining.
3. Tap **CFL.ca** to open the official box score, live tracker, or preview on cfl.ca.
4. Scroll down for **Game stats**, **CFL standings** (West and East), the **weekly scoreboard**, **recent results**, and **upcoming games**.
5. Tap **Refresh** at the bottom to reload immediately.

## What you'll see

A complete snapshot of the Elks' current game plus the league around them. The app refreshes on its own every 30 seconds while a game is live and every 5 minutes otherwise, so you rarely need to refresh by hand.
