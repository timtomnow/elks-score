Below the featured game and stats, two sections keep you caught up on where the Elks have been and where they're headed next.

## Steps

1. Scroll to **Recent Elks Results** — the last few completed Elks games, including pre-season. Each row has a **W**, **L**, or **T** badge, the final score, the round, and the date.
2. Tap any result to open its in-app box score.
3. Scroll to **Upcoming Elks Games** for the next scheduled games, each showing the opponent (**vs** at home, **@** away), the date, and the kickoff time.

## What you'll see

A quick history of recent games you can tap into for full stats, followed by the schedule of what's next.
