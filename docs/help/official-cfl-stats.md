The app covers the essentials, but the official CFL.ca pages carry the deepest stats and recaps. Every game links straight out to the right page for its state.

## Steps

1. On the featured game card, tap the **CFL.ca** button. Its label matches the game: **Live Game Tracker** while a game is on, **Box Score & Recap** once it's final, or **Game Preview** before kickoff.
2. From the weekly scoreboard, tap any game row to open that game's CFL.ca Game Tracker page.
3. The page opens in a new browser tab on cfl.ca.

## What you'll see

The official CFL.ca page for that game — full stats, play-by-play, and recap — separate from the in-app box score, so you can go deeper when you want to.
