When the Elks are playing, the top **featured game** card turns into a live scoreboard. This is how to read everything it shows you in real time.

## Steps

1. Open the app. While a game is on, the featured card shows a green **Live** badge with the current period and clock (e.g. **3rd · 04:12**).
2. Watch for the **gold dot** next to a team's abbreviation — it marks the team that currently has the ball.
3. Read each team's **record** (W–L–T), shown beneath its score.
4. Tap **Game details** to expand the round, kickoff time, and **Timeouts left** for each team.
5. Tap **Live Game Tracker** to follow live stats and play-by-play on CFL.ca.

## What you'll see

A live score that updates on its own — the card refreshes every 30 seconds while a game is in progress, so the score, clock, and possession stay current without you reloading.
